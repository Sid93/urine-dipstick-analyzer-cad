## 1. Schedule-G Required Label Elements

Per **Schedule G of the Medical Devices Rules, 2017**, every medical device label intended for sale in India must carry:

1. Trade name of the device.
2. Model / catalogue / reference number.
3. Name and address of the manufacturer.
4. Manufacturing licence number (issued by State Licensing Authority).
5. Lot or batch number.
6. Date of manufacture.
7. Expiry / use-by date or recommended re-evaluation date.
8. Storage conditions (temperature, humidity, light).
9. Special warnings or precautions for use.
10. The words "**For in vitro diagnostic use only**" (where applicable).
11. Country of origin.
12. WEEE crossed-bin symbol (per E-Waste Rules 2022).
13. BIS standard mark, where applicable (e.g., on the LiPo cell separately).

This document specifies how those elements are laid out across four physical labels.
